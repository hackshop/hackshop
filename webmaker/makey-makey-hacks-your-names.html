Challenge 3.2 Extend the MaKey MaKey

Now that you have mastered the basic contols, it's time to extend the power of the MaKey MaKey. Flip the board onto it's backside to reveal 6 additional keyboard buttons! WASDFG!

To connect these buttons, you will need to use the white, insulated wires that come with the MaKey MaKey and insert the "stripped" metal ends into the black plastic channels, or ports. You can also still use the same connections with alligator clips from the front-side on the back-side too.
