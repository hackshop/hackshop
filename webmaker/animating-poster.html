My Favourite Things

Family

So, my Dad is really embarrassing and my sister takes all my favourite things without asking. But that doesn’t stop them being amazing, and the most important people to me.

Cycling

One day, I’m going to be as wicked as Wiggins. I’m cycling all the way to the Olympics – that’s why my bike and I go everywhere together.

Literature

Books are amazing because they can take you anywhere. This month alone I’ve been on a boat with a tiger and had to fight for my school against a dragon.

Video Games

Zombies, spies, aliens, enemy soldiers, trolls, dragons or the other car thief. Whatever you’re up against, nothing feels more awesome than winning the game.
